Program Features

Spanish for Professionals®

48 Hours / 6 Months / Mentor Supported

Book your spot now!
  • Course Overview
  • Course Outline
  • Course Mentor

Spanish for Professionals is a self-paced, in-person and on-line occupational Spanish conversation course that seeks to bridge the communication gap between English and Spanish speakers. This course is ideal for executives, administrators, and anyone working with Spanish-speaking professionals.

Students will build their workplace Spanish vocabulary and conversation skills while learning native-pronunciations, building vocabulary, improving bi-cultural literacy, via readings, movies, and unique field trips, as well as cultural tidbits along the way.
