    Spanish for Professionals

    16 Hours / 6 Months / Mentor Supported

    Course Overview:

    Spanish for Educators is a self-paced, in-person and on-line occupational Spanish conversation course that seeks to bridge the communication gap between English and Spanish speakers. This course is ideal for teachers, administrators, and anyone working with Spanish-speaking students and parents.

    Students will build their workplace Spanish vocabulary and conversation skills while learning with pictures, native-pronunciations, readings, and quizzes, as well as cultural tidbits along the way.

    Course Mentor:

    Carlos Quinones, co-Founder of W3 Basics, is the instructional designer of all W3 Basics Spanish courses and materials. He is a native speaker with over 25 years of Spanish and teaching experience at the corporate training levels.

    Carlos has a BBA degree in International Marketing from Pace University. He has lived and traveled extensively in Latin America and Spain.
